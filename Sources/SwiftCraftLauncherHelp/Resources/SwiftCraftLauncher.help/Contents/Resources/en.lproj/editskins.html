Edit Skins

Swift Craft Launcher allows you to customize player skins, switch between skin models, and manage capes.

Upload Skin

Step 1: Open Skin Manager

Click "Skin Manager" to open the skin management interface.

Step 2: View Feature Options

The Skin Manager provides several options: "Switch Skin Model", "Upload Local Skin", "Cape Modification", and "Reset to Default Skin".

Step 3: Select Skin File

Click "Upload Skin", select a local skin file from your computer, then click "Open".

Step 4: Apply Changes

After confirming everything is correct, click "Apply Changes" to save your new skin.

Step 5: Confirm Upload Success

If the character avatar in the bottom left corner is replaced with your new skin, the upload was successful.
